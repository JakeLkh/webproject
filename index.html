The Solar System

The solar system consists of the Sun and everything that orbits around it, including planets, moons, and asteroids. It is a vast system that extends far beyond what we can see with the naked eye.

There are eight main planets in the solar system. Each planet is unique in size, composition, and environment. Scientists continue to study space to learn more about how the solar system formed.
